## 🎨 Creative Choices & Details

### 1. Color Continuity
Each image aligns with the **Obsidian Night** (`#0D1117`) background and incorporates elements of **Desert Amber** (`#E5A93C`) and **Crimson Surge** (`#E31B23`) through lighting highlights (sunset golden rays, dashboard telemetry screens, and modern vehicle paint).

### 2. Composition & Text Safe Zones
* **Hero Image**: The primary truck is framed on the right side of the canvas, leaving the left 30% empty of heavy visual detail. This ensures that display text and call-to-actions (CTAs) are readable on both desktop and mobile without overlay filters.
* **Warehouse & Fleet Shots**: Centered focal points with clean negative space.

### 3. Professional Photography Quality
The visuals mimic a high-end corporate photoshoot:
* **Lens**: Sony A7R V with 85mm prime lens for natural depth of field and perspective compression.
* **Lighting**: Natural golden hour sun vectors combined with cool high-tech LED highlights inside logistics rooms and storage facilities.
